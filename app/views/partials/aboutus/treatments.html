Nessa Smyth

Kathy qualified in the UK in 1999 with a first class honours degree in Physiotherapy and subsequently went on to achieve a Masters in Sports Physiotherapy and Manipulation from University College London in 2004. She has a wealth of experience working with athletes of all levels but also enjoys the challenge of treating and rehabilitating more chronic injuries that seemingly refuse to resolve.

  • London Olympics 2012 – Athletics
  • World Etchells Sailing – 2010
  • Davis Cup Tennis
